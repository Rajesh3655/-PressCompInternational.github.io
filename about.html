Our History

From Humble Beginnings
to Industry Leader

Founded in 1983 with a single stamping press and a team of five passionate engineers, Press Comp International began operations in Pune, Maharashtra. What started as a small tool room has grown into a multi-crore precision manufacturing enterprise trusted by India's leading OEMs.

Over four decades, we have continuously invested in the latest machinery, international quality systems, and human capital. Our facility now spans over 50,000 sq ft of modern production space equipped with high-speed presses, CNC tool rooms, and automated quality labs.

From telecom giants and automotive Tier-1 suppliers to electronics MNCs — our client portfolio reflects our unwavering commitment to precision and reliability.
