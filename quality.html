Our Process

Quality Assurance Process

A rigorous, multi-stage quality control process ensures only defect-free parts are shipped to our customers.

01

Incoming QC

Raw material chemical and dimensional verification before production release.

02

First Article

First Article Inspection (FAI) with full CMM, SPC, and PPAP documentation.

03

In-Process SPC

Statistical Process Control at defined monitoring intervals during production.

04

Vision Inspection

100% automated vision system inspection at press for appearance and form defects.

05

Final Audit

Pre-dispatch sampling inspection per AQL and outgoing quality audit.
