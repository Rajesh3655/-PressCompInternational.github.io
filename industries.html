Industries We Serve

Four decades of domain expertise across India's most demanding industrial sectors.

Industry 01

📡 Telecom & Networking

The telecommunications industry demands the tightest tolerances, most reliable contacts, and highest volumes — all areas where PCI excels. We supply precision stamped components for optical fibre connectors, cable management systems, network racks, and base station equipment.

Our reel-to-reel stamping lines produce millions of connector contacts and carrier-strip parts per month, making us the preferred supplier for India's leading telecom infrastructure companies.

Key ProductsSignal contacts, RF shields, housings, grounding clips
Key ClientsSterlite, HFCL, Tejas Networks, Vindhya Telelinks
Materials UsedPhosphor bronze, BeCu, SPCC, Stainless steel
Tolerances±0.01mm – ±0.05mm depending on part
Industry 02

🚗 Automotive

Automotive stamping requires a combination of rigorous quality control (IATF 16949), PPAP documentation, and the ability to handle high-tensile materials. PCI is fully compliant with automotive quality requirements and supplies to major Tier-1 and Tier-2 suppliers across India.

From structural brackets and body clips to precision electrical terminal stamping for wiring harnesses, PCI covers a wide spectrum of automotive metalworking needs.

Key ProductsBrackets, clips, terminals, shims, spring contacts
Key ClientsMinda, Motherson, LG Balakrishnan, KPIT
Quality ComplianceIATF 16949, PPAP Level 3, FMEA, Control Plans
MaterialsCRCA, HRC, Dual-Phase Steel, Copper
Industry 03

💻 Electronics

The electronics industry demands ultra-fine tolerances, miniaturisation, and high repeatability. PCI's precision die tooling and vision-assisted stamping systems can achieve tolerances down to ±0.01mm, making us a trusted partner for IC lead frames, PCB contacts, and surface-mount components.

Our clean and controlled stamping environment, combined with tape-and-reel packaging, ensures components arrive ready for high-speed automated assembly.

Key ProductsLead frames, PCB contacts, EMI cans, heatsinks
Key ClientsDixon Technologies, Salcomp, Wistron, Foxconn India
MaterialsCopper alloys, Kovar, BeCu, Al
PackagingTape & reel, bulk tray, custom carrier strip
Industry 04

⚡ Electrical & Switchgear

From busbars to switch contacts, PCI's electrical division supplies critical components for MV/LV switchgear, MCBs, contactors, relay devices, and energy meters. We have a long-standing partnership with leading Indian switchgear manufacturers including award-winning supply to Schneider Electric.

Silver-inlaid contact technology, precision copper busbar fabrication, and full traceability from raw material to finished part make PCI a reliable long-term electrical manufacturing partner.

Key ProductsBusbars, switch contacts, terminal bars, clips
Key ClientsSchneider Electric, ABB India, L&T Electrical
MaterialsETP copper, Ag-inlaid composites, Brass
AwardsSchneider Supplier Excellence Award Winner
