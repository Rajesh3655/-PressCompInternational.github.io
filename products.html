Our Product Range

Precision-engineered components for the telecom, automotive, electronics, and electrical industries.

Telecom
Precision Signal Contacts

High-reliability stamped copper-alloy signal contacts for telecom connectors. Tolerances to ±0.02mm. Available in reel format.

Telecom
RF Shielding Cans & Lids

Stamped tinplate and stainless steel EMI/RF shielding enclosures for PCB-level RF isolation in telecom equipment.

Telecom
Connector Terminals & Pins

Brass and phosphor-bronze stamped connector pins and socket terminals for OFC and telecom rack-mounted equipment.

Automotive
Structural Brackets & Clips

CRCA and high-strength steel brackets, spring clips, and retainers for automotive body and chassis applications.

Automotive
Electrical Terminals (IATF)

IATF 16949-compliant stamped copper terminals and connectors for automotive wiring harness assemblies.

Automotive
Spring Contacts & Shims

Precision stamped spring contacts, wave washers, and shims used in automotive sensors and actuators.

Electrical
Copper Busbars

Precision-cut and formed copper busbars for distribution panels, inverters, and switchgear — custom cross-sections available.

Electrical
Switch Contacts

Silver-inlaid and AgCdO stamped contacts for MCBs, contactors, and relay switches. Riveted and welded types available.

Electrical
Terminal Block Components

Stamped brass and steel current bars, clamping parts, and contact plates for DIN rail terminal blocks.

Electronics
IC Lead Frames

Ultra-fine stamped copper-alloy lead frames for semiconductors and IC packages. Tolerances ±0.01mm.

Electronics
PCB Press-Fit Contacts

Precision stamped press-fit contacts and SMT spring pins for PCB-to-PCB and PCB-to-housing connectivity.

Electronics
Stamped Heat Spreaders

Aluminium and copper heat spreaders and heat sink bases for power electronics and LED modules.
