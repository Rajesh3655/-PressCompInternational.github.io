Our Story

Engineering Excellence
Since 1983

Press Comp International has been a trusted partner in precision metal stamping and progressive die tooling since 1983. Starting with a single press shop, we've grown into a full-fledged manufacturing powerhouse supplying components to leading OEMs across India and beyond.

Our state-of-the-art facility houses high-speed presses from 25T to 400T, achieving tolerances as tight as ±0.01mm — meeting the most demanding requirements of the automotive, telecom, and electronics sectors.

🎯
Our Mission
To manufacture defect-free, precision metal components on time — forming lasting OEM partnerships built on quality and trust.
🔭
Our Vision
To be India's most respected precision stamping brand, globally recognized for innovation and excellence.
✅
Quality Policy
Zero defect manufacturing through IATF-aligned processes, continuous improvement and data-driven SPC systems.

What We Do

Core Capabilities

Comprehensive metalworking solutions — from die design to high-volume stamping — all under one roof.

🔧

Tool Design & Manufacturing

In-house progressive, compound & transfer die design and fabrication using precision CNC machining and wire-cut EDM.

  • Progressive Dies (30+ stations)
  • Compound & Blanking Dies
  • Deep Draw Tooling
⚡

High-Speed Stamping

Presses from 25T to 400T running at up to 800 SPM for complex multi-stage precision components.

  • 25T–400T press range
  • Up to 800 SPM speed
  • ±0.01mm tolerance
🔄

Reel-to-Reel Production

Automated coil-fed stamping for high-volume connector and terminal strip production — millions of parts per month.

  • Auto-feed coil lines
  • Carrier strip & lead frames
  • Tape & reel packaging
💡

Custom Engineering

DFM consultation, rapid prototyping, and value engineering solutions tailored to your exact specifications.

  • DFM & DFA Analysis
  • Rapid Prototyping (1–4 wks)
  • PPAP Documentation
