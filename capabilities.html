Our Manufacturing Capabilities

End-to-end precision metalworking — from concept and tooling to high-volume production.

Capability 01

Tool Design & Manufacturing

Our in-house tool room is fully equipped with CNC machining centres, EDM spark erosion machines, wire-cut EDM, precision grinding, and CMM inspection — enabling us to design and build complex progressive, compound, and transfer dies entirely at our facility.

This self-reliance gives us complete control over lead times, costs, and tool quality — translating directly to faster project delivery and lower total cost for our customers.

  • Progressive Dies (up to 30 stations)
  • Compound & Blanking Dies
  • Deep Draw Tooling
  • Transfer Dies
  • Forming & Bending Fixtures
  • CNC-machined components ±0.005mm
Capability 02

High-Speed Metal Stamping

Our stamping division houses a fleet of high-speed mechanical and hydraulic presses ranging from 25 Tonnes to 400 Tonnes. Operating at speeds up to 800 strokes per minute, we deliver high-precision complex components in large volumes without compromising on tolerance or finish quality.

Material capability spans carbon steel, stainless steel, copper, brass, phosphor bronze, BeCu alloys, and aluminium — supporting diverse customer requirements.

  • 25T – 400T Press Range
  • Up to 800 SPM Speed
  • Tolerance: ±0.01mm
  • Thickness: 0.1mm – 6mm
  • Materials: SS, Cu, Brass, Al, CRCA
  • 100% Vision System Inspection
Capability 03

Reel-to-Reel Production

Our automated reel-to-reel (coil-fed) stamping lines are purpose-built for high-volume production of connector strips, lead frames, and carrier-strip parts used extensively in telecom and electronics industries.

The automated coil feed, straightening, and levelling system ensures consistent material entry, reducing scrap and variation across millions of parts per month.

  • Auto coil-fed stamping lines
  • Connector strips & terminal banks
  • Lead frame production
  • Millions of parts / month
  • Vision & dimensional inspection
  • Tape & reel packaging available
Capability 04

Custom Engineering Solutions

Our application engineering team works closely with customers from concept to production. We offer DFM (Design for Manufacturability) analysis, rapid prototyping using our in-house tool room, and value engineering to optimize part designs for cost and performance.

Whether you need a fully engineered solution or just expert advice on an existing design, PCI's engineering team is your trusted partner.

  • DFM & DFA Consultation
  • Rapid Prototyping (1–4 weeks)
  • Value Engineering Studies
  • Reverse Engineering
  • PPAP Documentation
  • SPC & Process Capability (Cpk)
